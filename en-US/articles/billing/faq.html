    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    General Questions

    Q: What is the Billing application?
    A: Billing is a Business Central extension for contract management and periodic service calculation and invoicing.

    Q: Can it be used with both customers and vendors?
    A: Yes, the system supports both customers (outgoing invoices) and vendors (incoming invoices).

    Q: Can services be calculated automatically?
    A: Yes, the calculation worksheet can automatically generate calculations from active contracts.

    Contract Questions

    Q: How to create a new contract?
    A: Open the contract list, press "New", fill in required fields and add services.

    Q: Can multiple services be added to a contract?
    A: Yes, unlimited number of service lines can be added to a contract.

    Q: How to change contract status?
    A: Use the "Status" field menu in the contract card or the "Change Status" function.

    Q: Can a contract be interrupted and later resumed?
    A: Yes, use the "Interrupted" status and later change back to "Active".

    Calculation Questions

    Q: How often should calculations be performed?
    A: It depends on business needs - can be monthly, quarterly, or other period.

    Q: Can calculation lines be corrected?
    A: Yes, quantities, prices, and discounts can be manually edited in the calculation worksheet.

    Q: How to recalculate previous period?
    A: By generating new calculations or creating correction lines in journal with negative amounts.

    Q: Can calculations be grouped in one invoice?
    A: Yes, use invoice groups and grouping ID.

    Price and Discount Questions

    Q: Where are prices taken from?
    A: Prices can be from contract service, service card, or service price list.

    Q: How to apply discounts?
    A: Discounts can be defined in contract discounts or service sales discounts.

    Q: Can minimum or maximum amount be set?
    A: Yes, min and max amounts can be specified in contract service line.

    Q: How do price and quantity coefficients work?
    A: Coefficients multiply base value, allowing proportional adjustment of prices or quantities.

    Object Questions

    Q: Are objects mandatory?
    A: No, objects are only needed when service is related to specific object.

    Q: Can one contract have multiple objects?
    A: Yes, each contract service can have its own object.

    Q: How do object parameters affect calculations?
    A: If quantity source is "Object Value", then quantity is taken from object parameter.

    Posting Questions

    Q: Does journal posting create invoices?
    A: It depends on journal settings - can create invoices or only billing entries.

    Q: Can posted entry be cancelled?
    A: Cannot be directly cancelled, but correction entry with opposite sign can be created.

    Q: What is a billing entry?
    A: Billing entry is a posted entry reflecting provided service.

    Q: How to see if entry is already in invoice?
    A: Billing entry has "Invoice Ledger Entry No." field indicating related invoice.

    Technical Questions

    Q: What is system integration with Base Application?
    A: System integrates with customers, vendors, dimensions, number series and other standard functions.

    Q: Can data be exported?
    A: Yes, all pages support standard Business Central export functions.

    Q: Are reports available?
    A: Yes, system provides reports on contracts, calculations, billing entries.

    Q: Does it support multiple currencies?
    A: Yes, different currencies can be used in contracts and calculations.
